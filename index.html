The AI Reaction Assessment

Most organizations are navigating AI without much shared time to explore it together. As a result, reactions vary widely — curiosity alongside skepticism, excitement alongside concern.

This assessment helps surface how people in your organization are responding to AI. The goal isn't to judge those reactions, but to make them visible — so teams can have more honest conversations about where to experiment, where to slow down, and what questions still need space.

This assessment takes about 2 minutes.
