The AI Reaction Assessment for Organizations

Artificial intelligence is often framed as a technical shift. But inside organizations, the biggest barriers to adoption are rarely technical. They are emotional.

People experience AI through a range of reactions — curiosity, skepticism, fear, excitement, resistance, and moral concern. These reactions are not problems to eliminate. They are signals about what people need in order to engage with change.

Effective AI adoption requires more than new tools. It requires intentional change management — understanding the human dynamics that shape how people respond, and creating the conditions where productive experimentation can actually happen.

This assessment is designed to surface the emotional landscape in your organization and help identify where change management support is most needed.

The assessment takes about 7 minutes.
